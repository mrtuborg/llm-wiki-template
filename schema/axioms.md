# Schema: Axioms (Non-Negotiable Constraints)

These are fundamental invariants of the schema system itself.

1. **Single type invariant**: Every page has exactly one type. Types cannot be changed after creation.

2. **Source reference invariant**: Every page must have at least one `source_refs` entry. Pages without provenance are invalid.

3. **Domain membership invariant**: Every page belongs to exactly one domain. Cross-domain content creates cross-domain links, not dual membership.

4. **Template compliance invariant**: Pages generated by Ingestion must strictly follow templates. Deviation is a compilation error.

5. **Edge directionality invariant**: All semantic edges are directed. Undirected links are forbidden.

6. **Axiom immutability invariant**: Axiom pages cannot be updated by Ingestion — only by explicit human override. Axioms represent fixed truths.

7. **Decision source invariant**: Decision pages may only be created from conversational input. Automated extraction from raw documents is forbidden.

8. **Synthesis exclusivity invariant**: Synthesis pages may only be created or modified by the Synthesis Layer. All other layers have read-only access.
